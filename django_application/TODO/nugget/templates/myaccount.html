Welcome to the FAQ! Hopefully, the all the answers to your questions reside within this page. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to reach out to our developers at admin@mynugget.io.
  • favorite_borderHow did Nugget come about?
    Nugget is the result of a semester long project for a web programming course at the University of Massachusetts, Amherst. The OG team consisted of 6 undergraduate developers.
  • favorite_borderWhat is Nugget?
    Nugget is a virtual pet battledome, where users can customize their own pet Nugget and battle with other plays. You can shop for more items and increase your Nugget's statistics. Nugget is currently in Beta.
  • favorite_borderHow do I take care of my Nugget?
    You can care for your Nugget by feeding your Nugget, spending time in the Battledome versing other players. Nuggets love to eat, and they love to battle!
  • favorite_borderCan my Nugget die?
    As of now, your Nugget can not die. (That doesn't mean it won't change in the future!)
  • favorite_borderCan I change my Nugget?
    Would you change your child? I hope that answers your question.
